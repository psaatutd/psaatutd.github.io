    The University of Texas at Dallas




    The Pakistan Student Association is a proud dedicated organization created to unite all of the students at University of Texas At Dallas. Established in 2009, the organization aim is to raise awareness about our beautiful country and its traditional values. Every semester, we hold several events with a focus to bring unity amongst all students, faculty members, and the Dallas community by organizing cultural, social and political events. PSA of UTD aims to promote, celebrate and represent the Pakistani Culture, traditions and values. As an organization, our job is to bring a little bit of our home country to campus through our events and meetings.

    Chai Time

    Chai Time is an opportunity for student to come together and discuss Pakistani arts and culture. We will have this event every other week for the rest of the semester. There will be a different theme every week. Every week, we would discuss something unique about Pakistan. Hope to see everyone there!
